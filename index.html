satRdays are community-led, one-day, regional conferences inspired by SQLSaturday.

satRday in Cape Town

A satRday will be held in Cape Town during the first quarter of 2017.

The goals of Cape Town satRday are to

  • provide an affordable learning experience for R users;
  • support collaboration, networking and innovation within the R community;
  • demonstrate the need of such regional conferences

If you want to get in touch with the local organizing committee, please feel free to mail us:

  • Andrew Collier
  • Ryan Nel

satRday in Budapest

The first satRday was held in Budapest, Hungary, on 3 September 2016. There we two keynote presentations, six workshops and twenty contributed talks.

This event was made possible by support from the Budapest Users of R Network and financial contributions from the R Consortium and a number of other sponsors.
